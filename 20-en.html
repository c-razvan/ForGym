ABOUT THE CAMPAIGN

During December, your company pays corporate tax. Opt to allocate 20% of it to support gymnastics!

Join us in backing the collective endeavor to revitalize Romanian gymnastics. If you have a passion for gymnastics and wish to participate in our projects, your contribution will undoubtedly play a significant role in nurturing and safeguarding today's and tomorrow's generation of athletes!

About Sponsorships

The ForGym Fundation is an accredited NGO under ANAF with the registration number 99417553.
  1. For COMMERCIAL COMPANIES, the sums associated with sponsorships made are subtracted from the payable profit tax at a rate of 0.75% of the turnover or 20% of the profit tax owed.
  2. For MICRO ENTERPRISES, the sums linked to sponsorships are deducted from the income tax due for the fourth quarter at a rate of 20%.
